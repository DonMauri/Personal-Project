Christmas at Millenium Park!!

One of the attractions at Millenium Park during Christmas is the Christmas Tree. Tourists and locals can enjoy this attraction with loved ones. One can take pictures and walk around. People with different backgrounds can gather to enjoy this attraction.

People can enjoy live music. People playing live music can be found at different points around Millenium Park. Maybe it's something that you can enjoy!

There are also places where people can walk. Example of this can be seen on image. It can be a place where people can enjoy a walk.

Millenium Park is surrounded by tall buildings. Some are displaying Christmas colors. At night, it can be something beautiful to see. It can be a great time to take photos and enjoy what Chicago has to offer.
